Our Services

TARUN ENTERPRISES offers a complete range of railway construction and infrastructure solutions tailored to meet the demands of modern rail networks.

Construction: We execute end-to-end railway construction projects, including track laying, alignment, earthwork, and formation. Our team ensures structural integrity, quality control, and compliance with all regulatory standards.

Material Supply: We provide reliable and timely supply of critical construction materials such as ballast, concrete, sleepers, rails, and fittings—essential to maintain continuity in large-scale projects.

Petty Contracts: We handle small-to-medium scope contracts such as repair and maintenance works, minor track improvements, and site-specific civil tasks with the same professionalism as large-scale projects.

Electrical Works: Our expertise includes complete electrical installations for railway systems—ranging from wiring to substation and station electrification with certified safety protocols.

TRD Works: We specialize in all TRD (Traction Distribution) works including overhead equipment (OHE) erection, mast installation, wiring, and foundation work. We handle tenders and contracts for new TRD projects as well as maintenance jobs with full precision and engineering accuracy.

Our team is fully equipped to handle government tenders and turnkey contracts with a strong focus on safety, speed, and sustainability. From the ground to the grid—we deliver with integrity.
