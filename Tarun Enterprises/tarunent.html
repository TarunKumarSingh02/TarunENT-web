Welcome to Tarun ENTERPRISES

Tarun ENTERPRISES is a trusted name in the domain of railway infrastructure development, electrification works, and turnkey civil engineering projects. Since our inception in 2019, we have been contributing to India’s infrastructure growth through reliable execution of contracts and a commitment to engineering excellence.

We specialize in:

  • Railway Overbridge (ROB) & Underpass (LHS) Construction
  • OHE (Overhead Equipment) Works including mast erection, wiring, bonding & traction substations
  • Track laying, repairs, and alignment works
  • Supply of approved railway materials and components
  • AMC and periodic maintenance of tower wagons and traction machinery
  • Minor and petty contracts under zonal railways

Backed by a team of certified engineers, skilled technicians, and experienced project managers, we aim to deliver durable and safe infrastructure while meeting all technical and safety compliance requirements of Indian Railways and RDSO.

Whether it’s greenfield railway construction or modernization of existing assets—Tarun ENTERPRISES delivers with precision, speed, and accountability.
